Tornike Shengelia


From 2008 to 2010, Shengelia played for
both Valencia Basket and its
junior team, Valencia BC Sunny Delight.
In 2010, he signed with Spirou Charleroi
of Belgium, who later
loaned him to VOO Verviers-Pepinster for the 2010–11 season. In August 2011, it
was announced that he would play for Spirou Charleroi in 2011–12.
